Scenario Hunting is a design first test method. It comprises a series of steps that turn visual design artifacts (like Event Storming artifacts) to high-quality pieces of software. During implementation, scenario hunters' focus of attention is on design, behavior, and the effect of the scenario under implementation on the story from bigger picture.
